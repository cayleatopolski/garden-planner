Visualize your dream garden!

Grow Pro is a free online garden planner for simple and creative garden planning.

The easiest way to plan, grow and harvest your own food.

A popular question when starting a garden is what can I grow here and what can I grow now? We've worked hard to solve that for you.

Tutorial

Click "Build" to start!

Click the maginfiying glass on a plot to search for plants.

Favorite plants or add them right to your garden from your search.

Want a bigger garden? Click the plus above the garden to add more plots.

Click the trash can on a plot to delete it.

Don't want to search for plants? We have picked some of our favorite plants for you to add to your garden! Find them in the favorites section.
